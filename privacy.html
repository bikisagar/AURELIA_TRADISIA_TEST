Privacy Policy

Effective: 31 August 2025

Overview

Aurelia Tradisia (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal data, especially for our EU partners under GDPR.

What We Collect

  • Contact details submitted via forms or email
  • Business details necessary for proposals and logistics
  • Analytics data (aggregated, non-identifying)

How We Use Data

  • Respond to inquiries and provide proposals
  • Fulfill contracts, logistics, and installation
  • Improve site performance and content

Lawful Basis

Legitimate interests for B2B communication, contract performance, and compliance with legal obligations.

Sharing

We may share data with logistics partners, installers, or insurers strictly to fulfill your project. We do not sell your data.

Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, or as required by law.

Your Rights

EU residents have the rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port data. Contact privacy@aureliatradisia.com.

Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal analytics. You can block cookies in your browser. See js/analytics.js for implementation details.

Contact

For privacy requests: privacy@aureliatradisia.com.
